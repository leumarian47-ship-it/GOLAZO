Responsible Gambling

GOLAZO takes problem gambling seriously. Read this if you or someone you know needs support.

01

Our Commitment

GOLAZO is a free-to-play prediction platform. We do not accept bets directly. However, we link to licensed betting operators and take our responsibility seriously.

⚠️ Gambling is for adults only (18+). It should be entertainment, not a source of income. Never gamble with money you cannot afford to lose.

If you choose to bet, we urge you to do so within your means, with full awareness of the risks involved.

02

Know the Signs

Problem gambling can affect anyone. Ask yourself honestly:

If you answer YES to 2 or more of these, consider seeking help:

  • Do you spend more than you intended to bet?
  • Do you chase losses by betting more?
  • Have you borrowed money to gamble?
  • Does gambling affect your relationships or work?
  • Do you feel anxious or irritable when not gambling?
  • Have you tried to stop gambling but couldn't?
03

Safer Gambling Tips

  • Set a strict budget before you start — and stick to it
  • Never gamble to recover losses
  • Set time limits on your gambling sessions
  • Use deposit limits offered by licensed operators
  • Take regular breaks during sessions
  • Don't gamble when emotional, stressed, or under the influence
  • Treat gambling as entertainment, not investment
04

Self-Exclusion

If you feel your gambling is out of control, you can self-exclude from licensed operators. Self-exclusion prevents you from accessing gambling sites for a set period.

  • Romania — Contact ONJN: onjn.gov.ro
  • UK — GamStop: gamstop.co.uk
  • EU — Contact your national gambling regulator

All licensed operators are required to honour self-exclusion requests.
