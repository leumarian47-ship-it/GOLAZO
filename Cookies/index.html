What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the platform.

GOLAZO uses cookies and similar technologies (local storage, session storage) to operate and improve your experience.

03

Essential vs Optional Cookies

Essential cookies are required for GOLAZO to function. They cannot be disabled without breaking core features like login and preferences.

Analytics cookies help us understand how users navigate GOLAZO so we can improve it. All data is anonymised.

Marketing cookies are used by our affiliate partners to track referrals. These are only active if you click an affiliate link.

04

Third-Party Cookies

When you click links to licensed betting operators (Betano, Superbet, Bet365), those sites may set their own cookies according to their own cookie policies. GOLAZO has no control over third-party cookies.

We recommend reviewing the cookie policies of any external sites you visit.

05

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain GOLAZO features, including login and predictions.

06

Your Consent

When you first visit GOLAZO, you will be shown a cookie banner. You can choose to accept all cookies or only essential ones.

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by clearing your cookies and revisiting the site, or by contacting us directly.
